The Hungry Tide

A Novel by Amitav Ghosh

Book Report

Overview

"The Hungry Tide" is a novel by acclaimed Indian author Amitav Ghosh. It weaves a compelling narrative set in the Sundarbans, a vast archipelago of islands in the Bay of Bengal. The novel explores the lives of its diverse characters against the backdrop of this remote and ecologically rich region, highlighting the complex interplay between human activity and nature.
